Title: store

Description: This service is for retrieving store information, it is useful for extracting information about the count of stores, groups of stores on the basis factors like urbanicity, geography and stores within a particular distance. 

Example questions:

1. Find all stores of walmart in US   
2. Find the number of  stores of cvs in 1,3,5 miles of walgreens
3. Create 5 groups of Aldi stores on the basis of rural, urban, suburban, state and distance from Giant eagle
